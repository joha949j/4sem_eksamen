Røde Kors and Blonde Media

The We Are The Future-campaign pays tribute to the people, the youth, creativity, diversity and community, which the cool volunteers from the multiple Røde Kors stores help to create.

Focusing on the youth and caring about having a young work environment, is deeply embeded in the Blonde Media DNA.

We have never been scared of taking chances on younger employees. We believe that young people bring an energy, that fits perfectly with our ideals, such as, passion, work ethic and creativity. We also believe that the future of video, photography and design is in the hands of the youth and those people who really wants to work for it. It is from these beliefs, that our slogan "For the new generation" is born from.
