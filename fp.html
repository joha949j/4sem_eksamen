Us

Blonde Media is a creative production company and multimedia agency, specializing in branding and storytelling through film photography, social media and graphic design. Feeding off each other's fantasies, from fragments come future. This is where we live - in between, in the middle of it all, ahead of tomorrow. A visionary, passionated, young and creative team, who strive to create content that reflect our scandinavian origin: minimalism and simplicity, Blonde Media creates stories told in motion, moods, frames and graphics by minds that live on the spur of moment; now, tomorrow and beyond.

Strategy

We analyse and pose business critical questions. We are human centered in our approach and believe that relevance and purpose is based on insight driven and ambitious strategy.

Branding

We set direction and translate strategy into branding. We ensure a natural and meaningful brand presence that resonates with people, is easily understood and meaningfully differentiated.

Product and service

We engage with people and brands to understand their needs and aspirations, to better design products and services that make sense whether they are digital, physical or both.
